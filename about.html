About SDCC

Learn about our institution, mission and vision.

Our Story

The Sto. Domingo Community College (SDCC) was established to address the educational needs of the local community in Sto. Domingo, Albay. Founded as a response to the growing demand for accessible higher education, SDCC began as a modest institution and has grown into a beacon of academic excellence in the region. Over the years, it has expanded its programs and services to meet the evolving needs of students and stakeholders while maintaining a focus on community-driven initiatives.

Our Mission

To be a leading institution of higher education recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and community impact. Sto. Domingo Community College aspires to transform lives by fostering intellectual growth, critical thinking, and lifelong learning. Through a commitment to high-quality education, research, and outreach, we aim to empower students to become globally competitive professionals, ethical leaders, and socially responsible citizens who contribute meaningfully to the advancement of society. We envision a future where our graduates drive progress, strengthen communities, and uphold the values of integrity, excellence, and service.

Our Vision

Sto. Domingo Community College is dedicated to providing inclusive, accessible, and high-quality higher education tailored to the diverse needs of the community of Sto. Domingo, Albay. Our mission is to cultivate student success by offering rigorous academic programs, fostering a culture of continuous learning, and equipping students with the skills necessary for meaningful careers and lifelong contributions to society. We are committed to workforce development by collaborating with industry partners, promoting entrepreneurship, and preparing students for emerging job markets. Through active community engagement, research initiatives, and service-learning opportunities, we strive to address local and global challenges, ensuring that education remains a powerful catalyst for social and economic transformation.
